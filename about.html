About Us

A truly unique property, Patagonia Garden is the perfect setting for couples seeking an exceptional and elegant wedding experience. Perched high on a hilltop, our venue offers spectacular 360 views of the surrounding hillsides. Natural light fills our grand ballroom through our nearly floor to ceiling windows, and in the evening the sunset creates a perfect backdrop to your reception. With our soft gray walls, elegant wood flooring, 18’ high cathedral ceilings accented with dark wood beams, our venue is a blank canvas to create the wedding of your dreams.

While the ballroom will accommodate up to 400 guests, it is also perfect for smaller more intimate weddings. If you are a nature lover, the views through the windows will serve as the perfect way to bring the outdoors inside. The spacious lawns surrounding the venue create a private paradise for you and your guests. Cocktail hour or yard games on the lawn, invite guests to mingle while enjoying the views of sunset skies and sun kissed trees in every direction. Your guests are sure to be left with unforgettable memories and pictures to cherish for a lifetime.
